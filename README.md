## The Four Challenge Types (And Which One Is Right For You)

CFT currently offers four evaluation programs, each with a different structure, price point, and risk framework. Here's the breakdown:

### 1. Standard (2-Phase)
The classic. Pass Phase 1 (8% profit target), then Phase 2 (5% profit target), then you're in the live stage with an 80% profit split from the start. Daily loss limit is 5%, overall loss limit is 10%. Minimum 5 trading days per phase. Most beginner-friendly structure.

### 2. Ascend (2-Phase, No Minimum Days)
Same two-phase structure as Standard, but with **zero minimum trading days required**. Same 8%/5% targets. This is the pick for disciplined traders who don't want to be forced into trades just to hit a day count. Also the most affordable entry — a $5K challenge starts at just **$45**.

### 3. Accelerated (1-Phase)
One phase only. 10% profit target, 4% daily loss, 6% trailing loss. No minimum days. Once you pass, you go straight to the live stage. Costs a bit more than Ascend but faster path to funded status.

### 4. Instant (No Phases)
Skip the evaluation entirely — or rather, the evaluation is your live stage. You start trading immediately with a "double up" target, 4% daily loss, and 6% maximum trailing drawdown. Pricier, but for confident traders who hate waiting.
